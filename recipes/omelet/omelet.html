Omelet Recipe

Maybe the most simplest breakfast in the world. A good protein portion to star the day.

Ingredients

  • Eggs 2
  • Your favorite cheese 20g-30g
  • Salt at ease
  • Cayenne pepper at ease

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a mixing bowl. Whisk until just beaten.
  2. Drizzle olive oil into nonstick skillet. Pour eggs evenly.
  3. When the eggs look solid, evenly put the cheese over.
  4. Cheese will slowly start to melt, then Remove pan from heat.
  5. Carefully use a spatula to fold Omelet in half. Transfer to a serving plate.
